Trade Parameters
Understand trade terminologies
Breakeven Point (BEP)
The breakeven point is the point at which the market price is equal to the actual cost price of the trade, leading to no loss or profit on the trade.
Stop Loss
A price at which the trader wants to exit trade and restrict losses.
Risk-To-Reward
The ratio defined by the amount of risk that a person is willing to take for a certain amount of reward. A person willing to take a risk of ₹ 1,000 for a reward of ₹ 3,000, it means the risk to reward as 1:3.
Target Price
The price at which trader wants to exit trade and book favorable profit.
Entry Price
It is the price point at which you want to enter in a trade.
