While investing in any commodity or equity options, it is necessary that investors/traders keep a track of the price movement. If any investor or trader has to look for prices of these investment options on two different platforms, it will become a hassle for the investor and s/he may lose out on the opportunity to invest at the right time. Thus, to keep the investors right on track, we have a common platform for both.

After Market Order (AMO) is a type of market order that is placed by an investor after the regular market session is closed. AMO orders are placed on the exchanged at the opening of the next market session. The timing to place an AMO is between 5:00 PM to 9:00 AM for NSE
